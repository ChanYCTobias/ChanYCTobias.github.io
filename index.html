Product and Learning Websites

Clinical decision-support prototype

Ocsina / RetinaSignal

RetinaSignal is an OCT-based clinical decision-support startup prototype that converts routine retinal scans into structured biomarker summaries, risk flags, and clinician-friendly reports for neurology workflows, starting with multiple sclerosis.

  • Vendor-aware DICOM parsing for Heidelberg Spectralis and Zeiss Cirrus workflows.
  • Age-aware normative comparison, bilateral inter-eye analysis, and rules-based risk reporting.
  • PDF, JSON, and CSV export paths for expert review and downstream use.
OCT Clinical workflow DICOM Report export
  • Live demo: ocsina.com

Connected science curriculum

Corscientia

Corscientia is a learning website that maps core science topics across foundations, mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and computing, then turns each topic into a consistent study rhythm with interactive labs and review loops.

  • Curriculum graph for prerequisites, bridge topics, and local concept transfer.
  • Interactive lab surfaces for cell biology, kinetics, and science foundations.
  • Flashcards, quizzes, and concept checks built into the learning flow.

Modality Dropout in Privileged-Pose Manipulation (RL Paper)

This paper evaluates PPO policies under realistic pose outages (hard missing, freeze, noise+drift, and delay) and finds that dropout-aware training improves robustness more reliably than simply adding extra sensing modalities.

LeCal: Latency-Aware Dataset Curation and Alignment

LeCal is a practical post-hoc calibration workflow for LeRobot teleoperation datasets. It estimates action-to-state delay, reports confidence diagnostics, supports camera offset checks, and exports reusable alignment artifacts for downstream training.

  • Download my paper (PDF)
  • LeCal GitHub
  • Calibration dataset
