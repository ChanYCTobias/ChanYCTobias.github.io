Modality Dropout in Privileged-Pose Manipulation (RL Paper)

This paper evaluates PPO policies under realistic pose outages (hard missing, freeze, noise+drift, and delay) and finds that dropout-aware training improves robustness more reliably than simply adding extra sensing modalities.

LeCal: Latency-Aware Dataset Curation and Alignment

LeCal is a practical post-hoc calibration workflow for LeRobot teleoperation datasets. It estimates action-to-state delay, reports confidence diagnostics, supports camera offset checks, and exports reusable alignment artifacts for downstream training.

  • LeCal GitHub
